Core Security Controls

Encryption & Protected Transport

Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and protected at rest using modern, industry-standard encryption practices across managed infrastructure.

Role-Based Access Control

Access follows least-privilege principles. Team access is scoped to business need and managed through authenticated, permissioned accounts.

Secure SDLC

We use controlled code changes, review-driven development, and dependency hygiene to reduce production risk before release.

Auditability & Logging

Operational and application-level events are logged to support traceability, incident investigation, and change accountability.

Operational Hardening

We apply hardened environment baselines, security-aware deployment practices, and ongoing monitoring to strengthen service resilience.

Continuous Improvement

Security controls are reviewed and iterated over time as threats, platform capabilities, and customer requirements evolve.

How Statement Data Is Handled

WeAudit is designed to minimize exposure while preserving audit quality. Our workflow follows a controlled, traceable lifecycle from upload through output delivery.

  1. 1) Secure Ingestion

    Client statements are received through encrypted channels and processed in authenticated sessions.

  2. 2) Controlled Processing

    Statements are analyzed by our audit workflow with role-limited access and operational safeguards.

  3. 3) Findings Generation

    Audit findings are generated and reviewed in a traceable workflow to support consistency and quality.

  4. 4) Client Delivery

    Results are delivered through secure channels with clear summaries and supporting evidence.

  5. 5) Retention & Cleanup

    Data retention follows business and legal requirements; aging data is handled through defined cleanup practices.

Security FAQ

How do you protect uploaded statements?

Statements are transmitted over encrypted channels and processed in controlled environments with role-limited access and auditability measures.

Do you apply least-privilege access?

Yes. Access is scoped by role and business need. We avoid broad permissions and limit sensitive data access to authorized workflows.

How do you support customer security reviews?

We support procurement and security review workflows, and can provide additional documentation and questionnaire responses under NDA.

Do you share customer statement data?

Customer data is handled for service delivery purposes only. We do not sell customer statement data.

How often is security reviewed?

Security is continuously improved through periodic reviews, operational hardening, and platform updates.

Preparing for an enterprise review?

We can provide an expanded security packet, including architecture notes, operational controls, and procurement-friendly responses.
